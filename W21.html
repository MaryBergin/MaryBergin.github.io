About TransPlus


Since 1995 TransPlus has been helping transportation companies automate and manage their operations with a trucking software solution. TransPlus empowers fleets by giving them the tools to overcome current challenges, while being flexible enough to grow with them as their business grows.

TransPlus builds and maintains two solutions, Fleet Manager and Logistics Manager, as well as integration Modules that allow companies to customize their software. Their software is intuitive, designed to be user-friendly and logical. With a 97% success rate across their customer base, TransPlus is guaranteed to be successful with hands-on training and responsive support services.

My Semester Goals

  • C# Programing

    In the first half of this Co-op, I was tasked with managing our backlog of bugs and features to be implemented. As someone who was relatively new to C# programming, working on such a large software was a little intimidating. One of my goals was to become proficient in C# programming. Working on bugs was a great opportunity to get a more in-depth understanding of the code base as well as the use of C#. I was able to explore different areas of the code, look for interactions between different pieces, and work with software hands on. In understanding such a large code base, I was able to reach out to members of my team with any issues or roadblocks I faced. At the end of this Co-op I had a much greater understanding of C#, and while I may not be proficient I am definitely on my way there.

  • SQL Programming

    TransPlus utilizes SQL for their database of fleets and one of my goals was to become more comfortable using SQL. During my work term I was given the opportunity to utilize my previous experience and knowledge as well as gain confidence and experience working with a large database in SQL. Through defect fixing I learned a lot about querying and manipulating data, as the database was a key element for information on the functionality of certain features.

  • Eliminating Defects

    TransPlus has quite a large codebase and I found at the beginning of my co-op identifying the area of a defect was difficult. I made it one of my goals to be able to become more efficient in finding and eliminating bugs. I thought working with a large codebase would be a great opportunity to really work on my bug fixing skills. After getting a better understanding of the Software it was easier to identify the general area the defect was in, but with some practice and help from my team members, I was able to locate the defects in the code much faster. With the understanding of the software came a better understanding about how to fix defects, however I think there was still some room for improvement.

  • Writing Process Control Narratives

    During this Co-op I also wanted to be able to write comprehensive control system functional specifications, like process control narratives (PCN). Along with the station I programmed I was tasked with writing the Process Control Narrative prior to programming it. With the help of my supervisor I was able to comprehensibly understand the system I was to program, the specifications of the equipment, and write it in a way that was comprehensive and would assist in programming. I was also tasked with updating our current Process control Narrative template to be up to our standards. I can confidently say that I am confident in writing a structured and comprehensive Process Control Narrative.
