About ME!


Hi! My name is Mary Bergin and I recently completed my second year of Software Engineering with a minor in Media at the University of Guelph! I love music, movies, and sunshine!

I am currently in my first co-op position of Accessibility Assistant for the City of Guelph. Here I am helping to create their Web Accessibility project in order to reach AODA complience by 2021. I assist with document remediation and Web accessibility issues!

I am also very passionate about equal opportunities for all people, which is why, during my academic terms, I am an executive memeber of the Guelph Women in Computer Science Club. It was here that I helped to create a diversity in STEM workshop to bring light to the barriers that many minority groups face in STEM fields.
