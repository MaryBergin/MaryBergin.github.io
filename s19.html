About Guelph


The City of Guelph is a large community focused on integrity, excellence, and wellness. A municipality with many departments, Guelph’s City Hall is located on 1 Carden Street, which accommodates Accessibility – my department – planning, transportation, and many more.

With Guelph’s main goal being supporting the people, their slogan is “Making a Difference” and that’s what they strive to do in every department. With the AODA web content compliance date coming up in 2021, Guelph is one of the municipalities that is ahead of the game.

Fun Fact: The wire coat hanger was invented here in the 1920s!
